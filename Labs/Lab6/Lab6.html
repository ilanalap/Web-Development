Lab 6 Creating a Favicon

01/09/2022

By Ilana Laporte


What exactly is a favicon?

Favicons are the small icon that is next to the website on the tabs of a browser.


How are favicons created?

Favicons are created by scaling down the image that is intended to be used to 16x16 pixels.


How did I create the favicon for this site?

I created my favicon by deciding to have the first initial of my name as the image. Next I decided on the color scheme I felt would draw the eye. A background of gray and slow progression into deeper pinks. Then I searched the color codes and inserted them into the color pixelation freeplay on Code.org
