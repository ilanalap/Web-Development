Spoiler Free Review

In my opinion, this first movie in a trilogy that still stands up after nearly four decades. A teenager befriends an eccentric scientist, and they accidentally get stuck in the past using a DeLorean. Hijinks ensue, and they need to find a way back to their time. Even though the film is over 30 years old, the special effects still look great. The storyline is enjoyable throughout the film and makes you want to binge-watch the remaining two movies.
